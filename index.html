ABOUT SIBM

Nurturing talent since 1978, SIBM Pune was instituted with the core values of creating knowledge and influencing management practices. SIBM Pune has been ranked 13th in Management category amongst all the B Schools in India by National Institute Ranking Framework (NIRF), Government of India in 2024. We believe in collaborating and exchanging ideas to recognize the strategies that can be adapted to shape the future of the new hyper-virtual world and bring meaningful social change.

SIU has been ranked amongst top 50 Universities in India by NIRF, Government of India. The University has also been ranked among the top 250 Universities in Asia and top 150 in BRICS by QS World University rankings survey 2018. The institute was also awarded 'Gold' Star by the QS Star University Rating 2018.

ABOUT E-SUMMIT

The Entrepreneurship Summit is a dynamic event that brings together students, entrepreneurs, investors, and industry leaders to celebrate the essence of entrepreneurship. Focused on connecting minds and fostering innovation, the summit provides a platform for collaboration, learning, and networking.

Through a series of engaging keynote sessions, thought-provoking panel discussions, exciting pitching competitions, and interactive workshops, students will gain valuable insights and hands-on experience to fuel their entrepreneurial journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the entry free for all? Who can attend?

Yes, entry to E-SUMMIT is free for all. However, only college students are allowed to attend.

Can I participate in multiple events or participants?

Yes, you can participate in as many events as you'd like, provided you meet the eligibility criteria for each.

How can I register for the events?

You can register for the various events under E-SUMMIT’25 through Unstop. Please refer to the specific event page for detailed information. You can also register for events through the official E-SUMMIT website.

Is there a registration fee for participating in events or competitions?

No, the registration for all events is completely free of cost.

Will there be networking opportunities?

Yes! E-SUMMIT’25 is designed to facilitate networking among students, startups, industry leaders, and alumni. Events like the Startup Symposium and Startup Expo provide excellent opportunities for meaningful interactions.

Are there any specific eligibility criteria for participants in certain competitions?

All undergraduate and postgraduate students are eligible to participate. For specific details, please refer to the event pages on the Unstop platform.

When can I expect the results of the competitions?

Results will be announced immediately after the campus round concludes.
